Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is HyperX?

HyperX uses state-of-the-art encryption to ensure your conversations and data remain private and secure. We also offer two-factor authentication for an extra layer of security.

Can I use HyperX on mobile devices?

Yes! HyperX is available on both iOS and Android devices. You can download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How many people can join a voice channel?

Our voice channels can support up to 25 participants simultaneously, making it perfect for team meetings, study groups, or gaming sessions.

Is there a limit to the number of servers I can join?

There's no limit to the number of servers you can join on HyperX. You're free to be part of as many communities as you like!
